# Privacy Policy — Honest Risk Dashboard

*Last updated: 2026-07-06*

## The short version

**We collect no personal data. We cannot — there is no server.**

## Details

**The app.** Honest Risk Dashboard is open-source software that runs entirely
on your own computer. It has no user accounts, no login, no analytics, no
telemetry and no backend server operated by us. Your portfolio data
(`portfolio.json` and measurement history) is stored locally on your machine
and never transmitted to us. We have no way to see it.

**Market data.** The app fetches public price data directly from Yahoo
Finance, from your computer, using the open-source yfinance library. That
request is made by your machine in your name; Yahoo's own terms and privacy
policy apply to it. We are not a party to that exchange and receive nothing.

**Donations.** Voluntary donations are processed by third parties — Buy Me a
Coffee (with Stripe) and PayPal. Any personal or payment information you
provide there is handled by those companies under their own privacy policies.
We receive only what the platform shows a recipient (typically a name/alias,
amount and optional message), and we use it for nothing beyond saying thank
you.

**Email.** If you email us (myriskdashboard2@gmail.com), we use your address
only to reply. We do not add you to lists or share it with anyone.

**Cookies.** The app sets none. It stores two small preferences (language,
disclaimer acknowledged) in your own browser's local storage, on your machine.

## Changes

Any changes to this policy will be published in this repository, with the
date above updated.
